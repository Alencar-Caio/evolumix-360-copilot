## How It Works

### The RAG Pipeline

Evolumix 360 uses advanced Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology:

1. **Your Question**: You ask a technical question
2. **Document Retrieval**: The system searches relevant technical documentation
3. **Analysis**: AI analyzes your question with retrieved documents
4. **Validation**: Cross-checks response for accuracy
5. **Response**: Provides cited, verified recommendations

**Key Benefit**: Every recommendation includes sources, ensuring transparency and accuracy.

### Hallucination Detection

The system includes sophisticated hallucination detection to ensure:
- All claims are backed by documentation
- Confidence levels are clearly stated
- Uncertain areas are flagged for manual review
- No unsupported recommendations

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Q: The copilot seems uncertain about something**
A: This is by design. When the system isn't confident, it flags the uncertainty. Use your professional judgment and consult additional resources.

**Q: How do I export a report?**
A: Click the "Export" button in the report view. Available formats: PDF, Word, Excel.

**Q: Can I share consultations with colleagues?**
A: Yes, use the "Share" button to grant read-only access to specific team members.

**Q: How do I delete my data?**
A: Go to Settings → Privacy → Request Data Deletion. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

**Q: What if I disagree with a recommendation?**
A: You can flag the response and provide feedback. Our team reviews flagged items to improve accuracy.
